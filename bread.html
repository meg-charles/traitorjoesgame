The bread section at Trader Joe’s, where simplicity goes to die. It’s an area where you’ll find an impressive array of bread, but it’s also where things tend to get a little more complicated than they need to be. As you approach the bread shelves, you’re immediately greeted by a hodgepodge of baguettes, ciabattas, and whole-grain loaves.

To top it off, there’s a team member offering samples, which may be delightful for some but can make a quick trip to grab a loaf of bread feel like a prolonged shopping experience.

“Would you like a free sample?”
